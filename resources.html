Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve businesses across Gauteng, South Africa, including Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas. However, we can accommodate projects in other regions on a case-by-case basis.

How long does a typical installation take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small installations (5-10 access points or cameras) typically take 1-3 days. Larger enterprise projects may take several weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including 24/7 technical support, regular system health checks, firmware updates, and proactive monitoring. Our support ensures your systems run optimally at all times.

What brands and technologies do you work with?

We partner with industry-leading brands including Ubiquiti, Cisco, Hikvision, Dahua, Paxton, and many others. We select technologies based on your specific needs, budget, and long-term goals.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in seamless integration with existing infrastructure. Our team conducts thorough assessments to ensure compatibility and smooth transitions without disrupting your operations.

What is the typical cost for your services?

Costs vary significantly based on project scope, technology selection, and site requirements. We offer free consultations and provide detailed quotes tailored to your budget. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

Yes, we can arrange flexible payment terms for larger projects. We work with reputable financing partners to make technology upgrades accessible for businesses of all sizes. Discuss options during your consultation.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us via phone (+27 79 552 3726), email (info@logia.co.za), or our online quote form. We'll schedule a free consultation to assess your needs and provide recommendations.
