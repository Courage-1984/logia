Understanding Your Results

Download Speed

How fast you receive data. Important for streaming, browsing, and downloads.

Upload Speed

How fast you send data. Crucial for video calls, cloud backups, and file sharing.

Latency (Ping)

Response time of your connection. Lower is better for gaming and real-time apps.
